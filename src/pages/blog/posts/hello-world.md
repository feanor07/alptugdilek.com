I’ve been meaning to start writing for a while. Not because the world needs yet another engineering blog, but because writing forces you to verify what you know and think more clearly. AI tools make it easier to organize and structure ideas, but writing still forces you to think clearly and validate what you know.

## What to Expect

I’ll be writing about things I encounter in my day-to-day work: system design decisions, debugging sessions, and the occasional opinion on how we build software. Nothing groundbreaking, just lessons from daily work.

The bar for publishing here is simple: _Would I find this useful if someone shared it?_
If the answer is yes, then ship.

## Why a Personal Site

I also don’t have prior experience writing on existing platforms. So I thought, why not write in my own space, with my own rules?

This site is intentionally simple: static HTML, no analytics, no tracking. Just words on a page.

Let’s see where this goes.
