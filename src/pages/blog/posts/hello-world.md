I've been meaning to start writing for a while. Not because the world needs another engineering blog, but because writing forces you to think clearly. If you can't explain something in plain language, you probably don't understand it as well as you think.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about things I encounter in my day-to-day work — system design decisions, debugging war stories, and the occasional opinion on how we build software. Nothing groundbreaking, just honest notes from the field.

The bar for publishing here is "would I find this useful if a colleague shared it?" If the answer is yes, it ships.

## Why a personal site

I've relied on platforms for too long. Having your own space on the internet — one you control — still matters. This site is intentionally simple: static HTML, no analytics, no tracking. Just words on a page.

Let's see where this goes.
